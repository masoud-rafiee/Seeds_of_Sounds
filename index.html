Our Mission

Seeds of Sound is a multidisciplinary initiative by the Bishop’s University Computer Science Club. We retrofit vintage rotary phones with modern microcontrollers and AI so that every story told becomes a seed for a greener, more inclusive future. By blending nostalgia with innovation we aim to inspire action, preserve voices and cultivate community.

Bridging Past & Future

We give new life to analogue technology, turning rotary phones into gateways for digital storytelling and environmental pledges.

Sustainable Voices

Each recording is archived and nurtured like a seedling. Your voice becomes part of a growing digital forest advocating for change.

Open & Inclusive

Our platform welcomes everyone—no technical knowledge needed. Share your story, make a pledge and watch your impact unfold.

Future‑Ready

Designed by students and researchers, Seeds of Sound demonstrates proficiency in AI, IoT, hardware hacking and modern web development.
